## 1. Welcome

**Prompt Alchemist** is a hub for navigating the **agentic economy** together: an era where clear thinking, delegation to tools, and human judgment compound. Whether you are deeply technical or just getting started, the aim is the same — stay **relevant**, **informed**, and **empowered** as AI and automation reshape how we work.

This document is the **member-facing charter**: how we talk, what we optimize for, and how that connects to the longer-term mission. Strategy and execution detail live in Type 3.

---

## 2. What We Do — Three Pillars

### Learn — Stay in the loop

We share **signal-heavy** updates: tech and AI news that matters, notable events, and industry shifts — so you spend less time drowning in noise and more time understanding what is actually changing.

*(Aligned with Type 3: authority building, education, and proof through real breakdowns — not hype cycles.)*

### Master the craft — Prompting as the new way of work

We treat **prompting as structured thinking**: clarify intent, set constraints, break work into steps, evaluate outputs against outcomes. Members share patterns, tips, and real use cases so skills compound.

*(Aligned with Type 3: frameworks, playbooks, clinics, and repeatability — not one-off “magic prompts.”)*

### Shift the culture — Vibe working

We advocate for **vibe working**: using AI to work **smarter** and **flow better** — preserving energy for judgment, creativity, and relationships — instead of glorifying grind for its own sake. The goal is sustainable leverage, not burnout with a chatbot.

## 3. Ultimate Vision (North Star)

> **Build toward a micro-utopia where humans, AI, and robotics coexist, collaborate, and thrive** — with people in the loop where it matters, systems that augment dignity and agency, and technology treated as partners in solving real problems, not as replacements to fear or worship.

This sits alongside the **operational vision** in Type 3: a world where people are **intentionally skilled** at directing intelligent systems so results are **reliable**, not accidental.

**Primary:** founders and builders; product and engineering folks; analysts and operators who want repeatable workflows (marketing, ops, finance, etc.).

## 6. The Gap We Address

Access to AI is everywhere; **direction** is not. Most usage stays inconsistent because knowledge is scattered, “hacks” replace frameworks, and wins are hard to reproduce. We close that gap with **structure**, **shared standards**, and **culture** that rewards what actually works.

---

## 7. Community Rules & Norms

1. **No cross-promotion of other WhatsApp / Telegram groups** without **admin approval** (keeps trust and avoids spam).
2. **No harassment or bullying.** Disputes: contact an **admin** privately; admins will review and act as needed.
3. **Topics:** keep the conversation centered on **tech**, **AI**, **prompting**, and adjacent tools/workflows that serve the community’s purpose.
4. **No unsolicited business or marketing** (services, funnels, recruiting blasts, etc.) without **admin permission**.
5. **Good faith toward the community’s purpose.** Active **bad-faith** behavior or actions that **undermine Prompt Alchemist or its partners** (e.g. coordinated disruption, sabotage, or using the space against its mission) may result in **removal from the group**. We believe humans and AI can **coexist productively**; this is a space for people who want to **build** that future, not deny that these tools are part of the landscape.

*Admins may update these rules; the latest version should be pinned or linked where members join.*
